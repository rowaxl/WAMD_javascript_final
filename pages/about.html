What's this application about?

The TV show program's score – based on the opinions of hundreds of television critics – is a trusted measurement of critical recommendation for millions of fans.

The TV show program's score represents the percentage of professional critic reviews that are positive for a given television show.

This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.
